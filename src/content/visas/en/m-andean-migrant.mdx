## Who can apply for this visa?

For nationals of any of the States Parties of the "Andean Migratory Statute," applying the principle of reciprocity.

## Benefits and restrictions

### **Up to two years validity - only once**

This visa allows you to live and work in Colombia for up to two years. The total time granted will be up to the authorities. **This visa can only be applied for once.**

### **This visa admits beneficiaries**

With this visa you will be able to bring your family with you as beneficiaries.

### Open **work permit**

This visa allows you to work in Colombia for any company or institution.

### Time accrue towards residency**

You can apply for an R-visa if you accumulate two continous years holding this visa without losing it.

This visa allows the accumulation of time for the Resident Visa after a minimum period of two (2) years as a visa holder.

## Requirements:

### **1.  Criminal Record Certification**

Submit a criminal or police record certificate from the country of your nationality or where you've lived for the last five years. This document must be apostilled or legalized to Colombia translated into Spanish if it's in another language.

### **2. Statement of Intent and Support**

Write a statement detailing your reasons for wanting to live temporarily in the host country and how you plan to support yourself during your stay.
