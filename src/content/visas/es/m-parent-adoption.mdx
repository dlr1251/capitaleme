## Who can apply for this type of visa?

This visa category is suited “*For foreigners who are parents or children of a citizen who acquired Colombian citizenship through adoption*”.

The Colombian citizenship “*by adoption*” refers to the nationalization process of a foreigner to get the Colombian citizenship and is not to be mistaken with an [adoption of a Colombian national by a foreigner](https://www.notion.so/c62017b40829425a8d5e24e4122b7e31?pvs=21).

This visa is an M-type (Migrante) visa. This means that it allows you to accrue time towards residency and that you can loose your visa if you stay out of the country for 6 continous months.

## What are the benefits and restrictions for this visa?

### **Up to  three years validity**

This Visa allows you to live in Colombia for up to three (3) years. It also allows you to get a foreign ID card (Cédula de extranjería).

### **This visa admits beneficiaries**

With this visa you will be able to bring your kids with you as beneficiaries. That is, you can bring your children to live with you, once the main visa is approved.

### W**ork permit**

This visa allows its holder to work in Colombia.

### T**ime accrue towards residency**

This visa accrues time towards residency. If you have this visa for two (2) continous years, you will be eligible to apply for a Resident visa.

## Specific requirements

### 1. Letter of naturalization

The Visa and Immigration Authority will verify in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs database the letter of naturalization or resolution of registration of Colombian nationality through adoption of the parent or child of the applicant. If it is not possible to access this information, the interested party will be required to provide it.
When applying as a child of a Colombian citizen through adoption, the applicant must be under 25 years old and financially dependent on the Colombian citizen by adoption.

### 2. Kinship certificate

Authenticated copy of the Birth Certificate or Civil Registry that, according to the laws of the issuing country, proves the first-degree blood relationship between the Colombian citizen by adoption and the foreigner applying for the visa.

### 3. Request letter

Letter of request signed by the Colombian citizen by adoption, who is the parent or guardian of the applicant. If the applicant is a minor, the letter must be signed by the individuals with parental authority or guardianship.

### 4. Proof of financial solvency and income

Demonstrate financial solvency through average bank statements for the last 6 months and provide proof of income.
