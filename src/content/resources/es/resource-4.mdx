### Steps to apply for a "Salvoconducto" in Colombia

1. **Fill out the online application form** at [this link](https://apps.migracioncolombia.gov.co/registro/public/formularioRegistro.jsf;jsessionid=3C1428707C7A74945E91CD0A37AC4A69). Select the nearest Migration Colombia office of your current address.
2. At the end of the form, attach your a scanned copy of your passport merged with your last entrance stamp or your current visa which is about to expire.
3. **Save the FUT number,** which is generated after completing the form. This number is needed for your appointment.
4. **Schedule an appointment for the Salvoconducto at the nearest Migration office** using [this link](https://agendamigracol.naturasoftware.com/home).
5. **Attend the appointment** on the chosen date and time with your original passport, a printed copy of the passport's first page, the FUT number and the required documents, depending on whether the Salvoconducto is for leaving or staying in the country.
6. **Pay the government fee of 75,000 Colombian pesos during the appointment** for issuing the Salvoconducto.
