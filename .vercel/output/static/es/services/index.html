Need Backup with Migración Colombia?

Get a licensed attorney by your side when dealing with this public institution.

The Special Administrative Unit - Migración Colombia, is the authority in charge of controlling the borders and keeping a track on the foreigners in the country. They are the ones who stamp your passport at the airport when you enter and exit the country, and they are the ones who can give you a fine or deport you if you overstay your permits or visas. They are also in charge of Cedulas de Extranjería, Salvoconductos, Tourism Permit Extensions, among other functions.

As any public institution under the Colombian Democracy, all its public servants are obligued to comply and abide with the Colombian Constitution and Laws.
